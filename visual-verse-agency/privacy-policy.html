Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Visual Verse Agency ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through visualverseagency.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Contact form data: name, email, phone number, budget range, project type, deadline and project details you submit through our contact form.
  • Communication data: messages sent via WhatsApp or email.
  • Usage data: pages visited, device and browser type, collected automatically via analytics tools when enabled.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to project inquiries and provide quotes.
  • To deliver contracted services and communicate project updates.
  • To improve website performance and content based on aggregated usage patterns.
  • To send occasional updates about our services, which you may opt out of at any time.

Analytics & Tracking

We may use Google Analytics 4, Google Search Console, Google Tag Manager, Meta Pixel, Microsoft Clarity and LinkedIn Insight Tag to understand how visitors use this site and to measure the performance of our marketing. These tools may set cookies or similar technologies in your browser.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate this website (such as hosting or analytics providers) under confidentiality obligations, or when required by law.

Data Retention

We retain project inquiry data for as long as needed to respond to your request and, where a project proceeds, for the duration of our working relationship plus a reasonable record-keeping period.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at hello@visualverseagency.com.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information submitted through this site. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
